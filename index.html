Veej and Nitin

Once upon a time two avid bankers, Veej and Nitin saw each other at a regular training session. Nitin noticed her, but Veej being her usual self , was in no mood to notice anybody as it was her very important Sunday that was being wasted for the sake of training. However, a few phone calls and long whatsapp chats were enough for Nitin to get himself noticed. After solving few quizzes and puzzles, he finally won a date with Veej. Then they dated for few months and made cafes earn. They finally decided to take the relationship forward and tell their parents....and now are getting married....i.e on 21st of January 2016.

About Veej

She is as bright as lightning, with a heart of gold, ambitious, sweet and bubbly of them all. Loves adventure

She is a shopoholic. Take her shopping and she forgets it all. Nitin says that "Jo bhi mein kahun kam hi hoga!". She is the one who makes him feel alive, he feels wonderful when he is with her and misses her when she is not with him. She has a caring nature and has lots of love for everyone. She is Nitin's princess, life and soon she is going to be his wife!

About Nitin

He is shy, smart person and fun to be with. Loves anything that has aloo, from samosas to parathas. Gives a lot of space, one quality that Veej adores about him. A little short tempered at times...But loving too. The most important thing - something that Veej is too jealous of is his innocent face, people feel he is one of those "seedha bachhaa" which he totally is not.
